## About & Features

- **E-Ticket** is a website of booking tickets online.

- The website is built with React, Firebase, React-Router and React-Bootstrap.

- And has been used Authentication of [Firebase ](https://console.firebase.google.com/)

- There is a Homepage. In the homepage, it has a navbar and 4 boxes of tickets with ticket type, ticket price and a  buy button.


<a  href="https://e-tickets-booking.web.app/"><img  width="100%"  height="100%"  src="https://i.ibb.co/cxbdTXn/E-Ticket3.png"/></a>


- If you click on the buy button, you will be taken to the login page for **Sign up or Sign** in to view the destination page. Then you must be **Sign up** and **Login** to view the destination page.

<a  href="https://e-tickets-booking.web.app/login"><img  width="100%"  height="100%"  src="https://i.ibb.co/6HLbYqw/E-Ticket2.png"/></a>

- In the **Sign up** page, you will see a form for sign up. There are a name field, a email field , a password field and a confirm password field. And it has validation. If you type wrong email address or unmatched password it will warn you to correct this. And after you entered valid information and filled up the form, then you can **click on the Sign up button to Sign up**.  And you also can **Sign up with Google and Facebook**. 

- And If you already have an account, you can click on the Sign in text in the bottom of Sign up button or you can click on the sign in button in the navbar. And it will be taken to the **Sign in** section.

<a  href="https://e-tickets-booking.web.app/login"><img  width="100%"  height="100%"  src="https://i.ibb.co/1fjjh09/E-Ticket.png"/></a>

- In the **Sign in** section, you will get an email and password field. And you can logged in with your email and password. And you also can **Sign in with Google and Facebook**. 

- After you successfully Sign up or Sign in to the website, the **Destination** page will unlock  for you.

<a  href="https://e-tickets-booking.web.app/destination"><img  width="100%"  height="100%"  src="https://i.ibb.co/2M5nnMW/E-Ticket4.png"/></a>

- In the **Destination** page, there has a **Google Map** and a input field for search for you destination. You can enter pick from and pick to and also a date to search your destination. 
- After You click on the searchbar, You will see a search result.

<a  href="https://e-tickets-booking.web.app/destination"><img  width="100%"  height="100%"  src="https://i.ibb.co/7vp0nLP/E-Ticket5.png.png"/></a>

- Search result will show you the available tickets and the price of tickets.

- The website is dynamically  change route with url.

- And there is a **404 - PAGE NOT FOUND** section if anyone type wrong url that is not have any page in website.
